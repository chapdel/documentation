## Account & Dashboard

<details>
  <summary>How do I create a Notch Pay account?</summary>
  <div className="mt-3">
    You can create a Notch Pay account by visiting [business.notchpay.co/register](https://business.notchpay.co/register) and following the registration process. You'll need to provide some basic information about your business and complete the verification process.
  </div>
</details>

<details>
  <summary>How long does account verification take?</summary>
  <div className="mt-3">
    Account verification typically takes 1-2 business days, but it can take longer depending on the completeness and accuracy of the information provided. You'll receive an email notification once your account is verified.
  </div>
</details>

<details>
  <summary>How do I find my API keys?</summary>
  <div className="mt-3">
    You can find your API keys in your Notch Pay Business suite under Settings > API Keys. You'll see both test and live API keys. Make sure to keep your API keys secure and never share them publicly.
  </div>
</details>

<details>
  <summary>How do I switch between test and live modes?</summary>
  <div className="mt-3">
    You can switch between test and live modes in your Notch Pay Business suite by clicking on the environment selector in the top navigation bar. In test mode, you can use test credentials to simulate payments without processing real transactions.
  </div>
</details>

## Payments

<details>
  <summary>How do I accept my first payment?</summary>
  <div className="mt-3">
    To accept your first payment:
    
    1. Log in to your Notch Pay Business suite
    2. Go to Payments > Create Payment
    3. Enter the payment details and customer information
    4. Click "Create Payment" to generate a payment link
    5. Share the payment link with your customer
    
    Alternatively, you can integrate Notch Pay into your website or application using our [API](/api/payments) or [SDKs](/docs/sdks).
  </div>
</details>

<details>
  <summary>How long does it take for payments to be processed?</summary>
  <div className="mt-3">
    Most payments are processed instantly or within a few minutes. However, the exact processing time depends on the payment method and the customer's bank or mobile money provider. You'll receive real-time notifications via webhooks when payments are completed.
  </div>
</details>

<details>
  <summary>What happens if a payment fails?</summary>
  <div className="mt-3">
    If a payment fails, the customer will see an error message explaining the reason for the failure. You'll also receive a notification via webhook if you've set up webhooks for the `payment.failed` event. The customer can try again with the same or a different payment method.
  </div>
</details>

<details>
  <summary>Can I refund a payment?</summary>
  <div className="mt-3">
    Yes, you can refund a payment from your Notch Pay Business suite. Go to Payments, find the payment you want to refund, and click the "Refund" button. You can refund the full amount or a partial amount. Refunds are typically processed within 5-7 business days, depending on the payment method.
  </div>
</details>

## Transfers

<details>
  <summary>How do I send money to a beneficiary?</summary>
  <div className="mt-3">
    To send money to a beneficiary:
    
    1. Log in to your Notch Pay Business suite
    2. Go to Transfers > Create Transfer
    3. Enter the transfer details and beneficiary information
    4. Click "Create Transfer" to initiate the transfer
    
    You can also use our [API](/api/transfers) to programmatically send money to beneficiaries.
  </div>
</details>

<details>
  <summary>How long do transfers take?</summary>
  <div className="mt-3">
    Most transfers are processed within minutes, especially for mobile money accounts. However, bank transfers may take 1-3 business days, depending on the recipient's bank. You'll receive notifications via webhooks when transfers are completed.
  </div>
</details>

<details>
  <summary>What are the transfer limits?</summary>
  <div className="mt-3">
    Transfer limits vary by country, currency, and payment method. You can check the specific limits for each payment channel using our [Channels API](/api/resources#list-payment-channels). If you need higher limits, please contact our support team.
  </div>
</details>

## Integration

<details>
  <summary>How do I integrate Notch Pay into my website?</summary>
  <div className="mt-3">
    You can integrate Notch Pay into your website in several ways:
    
    1. **Checkout**: Use our hosted checkout page for a quick and easy integration
    2. **Payment Links**: Create shareable payment links without any coding
    3. **API Integration**: Integrate directly using our RESTful API
    4. **SDKs**: Use our official libraries for various programming languages
    
    Check our [Accept Payments](/docs/accept-payments) documentation for detailed integration guides.
  </div>
</details>

<details>
  <summary>Do you have SDKs for different programming languages?</summary>
  <div className="mt-3">
    Yes, Notch Pay provides official SDKs for:
    
    - JavaScript/Node.js
    - PHP
    - Python
    - Mobile (iOS and Android)
    
    Visit our [SDKs & Libraries](/docs/sdks) documentation for installation instructions and usage examples.
  </div>
</details>

<details>
  <summary>How do I set up webhooks?</summary>
  <div className="mt-3">
    To set up webhooks:
    
    1. Go to your Notch Pay Business suite
    2. Navigate to Settings > Webhooks
    3. Click "Add Endpoint"
    4. Enter your endpoint URL and select the events you want to receive
    5. Click "Save" to create the webhook
    
    Check our [Webhooks](/docs/webhooks) documentation for more information.
  </div>
</details>

<details>
  <summary>How do I test my integration?</summary>
  <div className="mt-3">
    You can test your integration using our sandbox environment:
    
    1. Switch to Test Mode in your dashboard
    2. Use test credentials and accounts
    3. Simulate payments and transfers
    
    Check our [Testing](/docs/testing) documentation for detailed testing instructions.
  </div>
</details>

## Security

<details>
  <summary>Is Notch Pay secure?</summary>
  <div className="mt-3">
    Yes, Notch Pay takes security very seriously. We use industry-standard encryption and security practices to protect your data and transactions. All communication with our API is encrypted using TLS, and we regularly undergo security audits and penetration testing.
  </div>
</details>

<details>
  <summary>How do I keep my integration secure?</summary>
  <div className="mt-3">
    To keep your integration secure:
    
    1. Never expose your API keys in client-side code
    2. Always use HTTPS for all payment-related pages
    3. Validate and sanitize all user inputs
    4. Verify webhook signatures
    5. Implement proper error handling
    
    Check our [Security](/docs/security/best-practices) documentation for more security best practices.
  </div>
</details>
