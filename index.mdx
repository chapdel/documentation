## What can you do with Notch Pay?

- **Accept payments** via Mobile Money, bank cards, and other popular methods in Africa
- **Make money transfers** to beneficiaries quickly and securely
- **Easily integrate** payment services into your applications using our RESTful API
- **Manage your transactions** through our intuitive dashboard
