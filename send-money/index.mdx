Notch Pay makes it easy to send money to individuals and businesses across Africa. Whether you need to pay suppliers, reimburse expenses, or disburse salaries, our platform provides reliable and secure money transfer services.

## Overview

With Notch Pay's Transfers capabilities, you can:

- Send money to mobile money accounts and bank accounts
- Create and manage recipients for recurring transfers
- Schedule one-time or recurring transfers
- Track the status of your transfers in real-time
- Receive notifications when transfers are completed

Transfers in Notch Pay refer to the process of distributing funds to recipients, which includes:

- **Payouts**: Sending money to customers, vendors, or employees
- **Withdrawals**: Moving funds from your Notch Pay account to your own accounts
- **Fund Distributions**: Distributing funds to multiple recipients, such as marketplace sellers

## Getting Started

To start using Notch Pay Transfers:

1. [Create a Notch Pay account](https://business.notchpay.co/register) if you don't have one
2. Complete your business profile and verification
3. Add funds to your Notch Pay account
4. Create recipients or use existing ones
5. Initiate transfers to your recipients

## Transfer Flow

The typical transfer flow with Notch Pay works as follows:

1. **Create Recipient**: Add the recipient's details to your recipients list (optional but recommended for recurring transfers)
2. **Initiate Transfer**: Specify the amount, currency, and recipient details
3. **Validation**: Notch Pay validates the transfer details and recipient information
4. **Processing**: The transfer is processed through the appropriate payment network
5. **Status Updates**: Notch Pay updates the transfer status and sends notifications
6. **Completion**: The recipient receives the funds in their account

## Managing Recipients

Recipients (also known as beneficiaries) are individuals or businesses that you send money to. By creating and managing recipients, you can:

- Save recipient details for future transfers
- Organize recipients by categories or tags
- Track transfer history for each recipient
- Ensure accurate and consistent recipient information

## Security Measures

Notch Pay implements several security measures to protect your transfers:

- **Two-Factor Authentication**: Additional verification for high-value transfers
- **Transfer Limits**: Customizable limits to control transfer amounts
- **Activity Monitoring**: Real-time monitoring of transfer activity
- **Fraud Detection**: Advanced algorithms to detect and prevent fraudulent transfers
- **Secure API**: Encrypted communication for all transfer-related operations

## Fees and Limits

Transfer fees vary depending on:

- The transfer method (mobile money, bank transfer)
- The recipient's country and currency
- The transfer amount

Transfer limits are determined by:

- Your account verification level
- Regulatory requirements in the recipient's country
- The transfer method

For detailed information about fees and limits, please visit our [pricing page](https://notchpay.co/pricing).

## Integration Options

You can send money through Notch Pay using:

- **Dashboard**: Use our web dashboard to manually initiate transfers
- **API**: Integrate with our [Transfers API](/api/transfers) for programmatic transfers
- **SDKs**: Use our official libraries for various programming languages
